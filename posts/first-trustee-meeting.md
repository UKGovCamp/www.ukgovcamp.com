Hi everyone,

An exciting day for us at UKGovCamp: this Wednesday, we had our first official meeting of our new board of trustees. We’ll be writing more about this group in the near future. It’s made up of the following people:

* Angela Hilton
* [Daisy Wu](https://daisywu-design.framer.website/)
* [David Durant](https://bowsy.co.uk/)
* [Manny Ibiayo](https://www.f5innovation.com/about-us.html)
* [Mark Delgarno](https://createchange.io/)
* [Richard Stirling](https://oxfordinsights.com/team/richard-stirling/)

We discussed the following items:

* Feedback from the January event
* Our amazing archive of unconference session documents being used by an academic researcher
* Grant funding - specifically to [OneTeamGov](https://www.oneteamgov.uk/) and [CharityCamp](https://www.ukcharitycamp.com/)
* Finalising the Articles of Association we will use to turn UKGovCamp into an official Company Limited by Guarantee
* Updating the digital tools we use
* Future ways we might support the broader GovCamp community between the big January events

Lots to be going on with!

The UK Gov Camp team.
